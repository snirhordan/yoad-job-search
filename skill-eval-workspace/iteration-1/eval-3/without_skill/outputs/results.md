# Remote Software Developer Positions on Ashby-Hosted Job Boards

**For:** Yoad, fresh CS grad looking to work remotely from Israel
**Date:** March 28, 2026
**Source:** Ashby-hosted job boards (jobs.ashbyhq.com)

---

## Summary

I scanned dozens of Ashby-hosted company job boards for remote software developer positions suitable for a fresh CS graduate working from Israel. The reality is challenging: most "new grad" roles on Ashby are US-based and on-site/hybrid, and most truly remote positions require 3+ years of experience. Israel is not a common hiring location for these companies.

Below I categorize findings by how realistic they are for Yoad.

## Recommendations for Yoad

1. **Apply broadly to EMEA remote roles** even if they say "senior" -- Deel, Supabase, WunderGraph, and G2i all hire in EMEA which technically includes Israel. Some companies are flexible on experience for strong candidates.

2. **Top picks to apply to right now:**
   - Deel Full Stack Engineer (EMEA) -- even though it says 4+ years, Deel has Israel infrastructure
   - G2i contract roles -- they're a talent marketplace with global remote positions
   - Supabase SDK Engineer roles -- fully remote, global hiring
   - Checkly -- watch for new junior openings (they hire in UTC-3 to UTC+3 which covers Israel)
   - LangChain Customer Engineer (Europe)

3. **Build a pipeline:** Set up alerts on these Ashby job boards:
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/deel
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/supabase
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/checkly
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/posthog
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/ashby
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/g2i
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/linear
   - https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/wundergraph

4. **Reality check:** The Ashby ecosystem skews heavily toward US-based companies. For a fresh grad working remotely from Israel, the best strategy may be to also look at:
   - Israeli companies using Ashby (like Zip's Israel office)
   - European remote-first companies
   - Companies explicitly hiring in EMEA/worldwide

5. **Quora is NOT an option** -- despite being remote-first with new grad positions, Israel is not on their eligible countries list.
